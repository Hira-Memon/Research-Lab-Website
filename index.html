About Us

Electric power systems are critical infrastructures for modern society. Our laboratory has been and will be dedicated to building a resilient, intelligent, sustainable, and smart power grid, and providing solutions not only technically and economically sound, but also satisfying social, environmental, and business development requirements.


Power Systems Research Laboratory(PSRL) estabalished at Pakistan Navy Engineering College, a consitiuent college of National Univeristy of Sciences and Technology, is a multi-use facility for both research and educational activities. The lab provides opportunities for PhD, Master's and undergraduate students of the NUST PNEC to perform advanced work in the areas of micro-grid, smart grid technologies, power electronic devices and converters, renewable energy systems and integration, controls and communications, automation and relaying, distribution engineering, and other emerging electric power technology areas.
